## Tier Classification & Usage

### 1. Frontier Efficiency
These are state-of-the-art lightweight models designed to provide reasoning capabilities previously only possible in much larger "Pro" or "Ultra" models.
- **Key Model:** `gemini-3-flash-preview`
- **Best For:** Coding assistants, complex multi-step agents, and scientific reasoning tasks.
- **Focus:** High intelligence at a fraction of the cost of flagship Pro models.

### 2. Flagship Open Weights
Models with publicly released weights, allowing for private hosting and extensive customization.
- **Key Model:** `gemma-4-31b-it`
- **Best For:** Private local hosting, fine-tuning on sensitive corporate data, and academic research.
- **Focus:** Maximum performance for local/private deployments.

### 3. High-Efficiency / Optimized
Designed for high-volume tasks where speed and cost are critical. Often employs Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures to minimize active parameters.
- **Key Models:** `gemini-3.1-flash-lite-preview`, `gemma-4-26b-a4b-it`
- **Best For:** Large-scale summarization, basic chat bots, and high-throughput data extraction.
- **Focus:** Scalability and near-zero cost.

### 4. Legacy Tiers
Models from previous major release cycles maintained for stability and backward compatibility.
- **Key Models:** `gemini-2.5-flash`, `gemini-2.5-flash-lite`
- **Best For:** Maintaining existing production pipelines and applications built on 2.5-specific behaviors.
- **Focus:** Long-term stability for legacy applications.
